Who Am I?

Hi- I'm Kevyn. I do full-stack software development. I graduated from The New Jersey Institute of Technology with a BS in Computer Science and a minor in Web & Information Systems. I'm currently leading the front-end development of an upcoming ecommerce platform, Giftapart!

Throughout my degree and early career, my focus was on server-side development in languages such as Java, Python, and C++. I've since extended my front-end skills after becoming fluent with the Angular Framework. Because much of my knowledge (and enjoyment of programming) lies within concepts such as Data Structures, Algorithms, and OOP, I believe that I offer a unique and valuable perspective to full-stack development.
