My Story

I've been a programmer since I was in 8th grade- but I didn't realize that until much later.

When I was in middle school, I became fascinated with the idea of creating my own video game. I had no programming experience, but I found a tool online called Game Maker. The program had a drag-and-drop interface to help non-programmers get started, but every command had a "Game Maker Language" equivalent. Once the user was comfortable, they could shift the project entirely to code. This is how I began to inadvertently learn some of the fundamentals of programming.

In high school, I took a Computer Science elective and found that I recognized a lot of the core concepts. I enjoyed applying the knowledge I had gained on my own, and I soon fell in love with the problem-solving, puzzle-like nature of programming. This is what ultimately led to my decision to major in Computer Science.

Hi! I'm Kevyn Jaremko- a 22 year old, recent college graduate with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and a minor in Web and Information Systems. I've created this site as a portfolio to showcase my experience and the projects I've worked on. A big hobby of mine is game design, which is what originally fostered my interest in programming.
